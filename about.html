Who we are

Litro Gas Lanka is Sri Lanka’s national LPG provider providing LPG as a clean, green energy to over 04 million Sri Lankans.At a national level, Litro Gas fulfills the LPG requirements of households, businesses and commercial ventures across various spectrums in the country.

maintains a vibrant presence in the Sri Lankan energy sector. Modern LPG storage facilities based at the Kerawalapitiya terminal and the state-of-the-art storage and bottling facility at Mabima Sapugaskande, recognized as one of the largest in the region,fortifies the Company’s presence as the national LPG provider.

Litro Gas also operates a storage facility in Hambantota, giving it an edge in managing a seamless LPG delivery process across the country.
